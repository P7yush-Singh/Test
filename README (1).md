# BusinessOS AI -- Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Vision

BusinessOS AI is an all-in-one SaaS platform that helps offline and SMB
businesses manage their entire operation from a single dashboard. The
platform combines website management, ecommerce, bookings, CRM,
inventory, finance, marketing automation, AI assistants, analytics, and
customer engagement.

## Goals

-   Replace multiple disconnected tools with one platform.
-   Provide industry-specific modules on a shared core.
-   Generate recurring subscription revenue.
-   Support white-label deployments and custom domains.
